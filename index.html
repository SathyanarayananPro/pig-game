🎯Rules

How to Play ?

🎲 Roll the Dice:

  • On your turn, click the Roll button.
  • The dice shows a number from 1 to 6.

➡️ Add to Your Turn Score:

  • If you roll 2, 3, 4, 5, or 6, the number is added to your current score.
  • You can keep rolling to increase your score.

⚡ But Beware!

  • If you roll a 1, you lose all points collected in your turn, and your turn ends immediately!

✋ Hold Your Score:

  • If you click the Hold button, your current turn score is added to your total score, and your turn ends.
  • Now it's the next player's turn.

🏆 Winning the Game:

  • The first player to reach or exceed 100 points wins!
